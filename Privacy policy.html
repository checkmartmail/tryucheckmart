Privacy policy

How CheckMart handles your data

Updated on 4 November,2023

Introduction

Welcome to CheckMart, a trusted online platform dedicated to enhancing your shopping experience. This comprehensive Privacy Policy outlines the data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect it. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your personal information. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your information.


Data Collection and Usage

At CheckMart, we collect various types of user data to provide you with a personalized and efficient shopping experience. The data we collect includes your name, gender, phone number, and a securely encoded password, which cannot be read by anyone, including CheckMart members.


Local Data Storage

Some data, such as your product viewing history, is stored locally on your device, not on CheckMart's servers. This data is used to provide you with a list of products you've previously viewed, making it easier for you to make informed decisions.


How We Collect Data

We collect user data in the following ways:

Registration

When you sign up on our website, you provide us with your name, gender, phone number, and a secure password.

Product Viewing

Your product viewing history is collected when you visit product pages and use the "bag" option to save products for later use or purchase.


Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your data primarily to enhance your CheckMart experience, including but not limited to:

Personalization

Your data helps us personalize your shopping experience by offering tailored product recommendations.

User Activities

We store your data for tracking your activities on our platform, enabling you to review and retrace your past interactions with our website.


Data Security

At CheckMart, we take data security very seriously. Your sensitive data, such as your phone number and password, is stored in an encrypted form that is unreadable by anyone, including our own team members. We employ robust security measures to safeguard your information.


Data Deletion

We respect your right to control your data. Local data, such as product viewing history, can be deleted by clearing your site data. To remove data from CheckMart's database, you can utilize the "Delete Account" feature, which is accessible via the three dots next to the "Log Out" option on your Account page.


Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. In the event that we intend to share data with organizations like Amazon or Google in the future, we will seek your explicit permission and provide prior notifications.


Protecting Your Password

Your password is crucial to maintaining the security of your account. We strongly advise you to never share your password with anyone.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is subject to updates and modifications. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website. It is your responsibility to regularly review this policy to stay informed about how we handle your data.


Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. These technologies help us analyze user behavior, improve website performance, and provide a more personalized experience.


Advertising and Affiliate Marketing

CheckMart may display advertisements and utilize affiliate marketing to generate revenue. These ads may use tracking technologies to provide relevant content and generate advertising income.


Consent and Permissions

By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your data as outlined in this policy. If we intend to use your data for purposes not described in this policy, we will request your explicit consent.


Children's Privacy

CheckMart is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store data from individuals under the age of 13.


Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at Contact details provided below


Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or simply want to get in touch, please feel free to contact us through our Contact Us Page. We value your feedback and look forward to hearing from you.
